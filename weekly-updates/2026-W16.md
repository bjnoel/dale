Back from two weeks off. Dale has been on strike the whole time because I didn't leave a weekly update behind, which is exactly how the gate is supposed to work. Not going to backfill W15, the vacation speaks for itself.

## What actually got done

Published [AI is like SEO](/posts/ai-is-like-seo/), a short piece arguing that developers resent AI for the same reason they resent SEO: a black-box discipline with rules that change without notice. The core claim is that both the sceptic engineer and the bullish business leader are reacting to the same underlying fear, just wearing different hats. First blog post in a while, and the first one written with the editor-in-the-loop workflow I've been meaning to try: stream-of-consciousness draft, then ask Claude what to cut and sharpen, then iterate. Worked well. The published piece is a third of the length of the first draft and makes one clear point instead of four foggy ones.
