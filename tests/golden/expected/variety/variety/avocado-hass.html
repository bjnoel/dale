Buy Avocado - Hass Trees in Australia

Updated DATE · 2 nurseries tracked · 2 in stock

Hass is the world's most widely grown avocado, traced to a single seedling raised by postman Rudolph Hass at La Habra Heights, California in 1926 and patented in 1935. Every commercial Hass tree descends from grafts of that one mother tree.

It is a Type A flowering variety. The fruit has pebbly skin that turns from dark green to purplish-black as it ripens, with rich, nutty, high-oil flesh that yields to gentle pressure when ready.
