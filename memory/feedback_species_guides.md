Benedict flagged that the sapodilla growing guide (DAL-39) claimed "In Australia, Tikal is most commonly available" but he had never seen Tikal listed anywhere.

When writing species growing guides, ONLY mention specific named varieties if they appear in the current tracked nursery data (fruit_species.json or actual scraped products). Do not source variety information from general web knowledge without cross-referencing with our actual data.

The corrected sapodilla guide now mentions Krasuey, Ponderosa, and Sawo Manilla (which are actually tracked by Ross Creek Tropicals).
