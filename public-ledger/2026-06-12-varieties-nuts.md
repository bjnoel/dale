# 2026-06-12 — Variety blurbs for nut species (almond, pistachio, walnut)

Extended the treestock per-variety "what's unique" blurb layer to the nut catalogue.

- **Almond:** 15 varieties described (All In One, Mission, IXL, Johnston Prolific, Indian,
  Nonpareil, Monterey, Price, Ne Plus Ultra, Fritz Paper Shell, All-in-One Zaione Paper Shell,
  Vela, Bitter, Carmel, Chellaston), 10 skipped.
- **Pistachio:** 3 described (Sirora female under two listing spellings, plus Chinese pistache
  clarified as an ornamental, not the edible nut), 4 skipped.
- **Walnut:** 5 described (black walnut and English/common walnut, each under two listing
  spellings, plus the historic Placentia), 0 skipped.

Totals: 23 varieties added, 14 skipped. Every blurb is verified against at least two reputable
sources with at least one independent (non-nursery) reference. Two name traps were corrected for
buyers: "Indian almond" is Terminalia catappa (a tropical tree, not a true almond) and "Chinese
pistachio" is Pistacia chinensis (an ornamental and rootstock, not the edible pistachio).
