# 2026-06-11 — Variety blurbs: macadamia + pecan (rollout window)

Added verified "what's unique" descriptions to the treestock `/variety` pages for two nut
species in the DEC-178 rollout.

- **Macadamia** (new file): 24 varieties described, 9 skipped. Covers the HAES numbered
  selections (246/Keauhou, 344/Kau, 741/Mauka, 814, 816, 849), the Hidden Valley A-series
  (A4, A16, A38, A203, A268), Beaumont, Daddow, the H2 rootstock, the M. tetraphylla type,
  and the home-garden cultivars Pinkalicious, MiniMaca and Lotsa Nuts (with their nursery
  alias listings).
- **Pecan**: 14 more varieties described (Apache, Cape Fear, Cherokee, Cheyenne, Desirable,
  Kiowa, Mohawk, Pabst, Riverside, Shoshoni, Tejas), joining Pawnee and Wichita. No new skips.

Sourced against the Hawaii Agricultural Experiment Station bulletin, Queensland DPI, HortScience,
the USDA-ARS Carya cultivar database, US university pecan extension services, plant patents, ACRA
and the RFCA archive. Skips were thin-source cultivars or generic nursery listings, never guesses.
Pecan "SP" (self pollinating) listing tags were corrected to the verified pollination type on each
page. Macadamia and pecan are both complete (0 remaining) after this window. Tests green; not yet
deployed (deploy happens at the serialized rollout close-out).
