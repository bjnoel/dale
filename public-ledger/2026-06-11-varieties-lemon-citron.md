# 2026-06-11 (variety rollout window: lemon, citron)

Added verified "what's unique" blurbs for 18 more varieties on treestock.com.au variety pages:
13 lemon (Eureka Seedless, Myer, Prior Lisbon, Villa Franca, Eureka Variegated and Variegated
Pink, the Yuzu spelling variants, Lemonicious, Pinky, Lots O' Lemons, and the Lemon Heaven
seedless Eureka pair) and 5 citron (Buddha's Hand in its three listed spellings, Etrog, Yuzu).

Every fact was checked against at least two reputable sources, leaning on the UC Riverside
Citrus Variety Collection, UF/IFAS, and Auscitrus. 22 slugs were skipped on purpose: two
(Thornless, Limoncello) because only recycled nursery marketing copy exists for them, and
twenty that are not lemon or citron varieties at all (herbs like lemon balm and lemon verbena,
ornamentals, multigraft products, and product-title parsing noise). Skips are recorded so
future runs do not re-attempt them.

With this, every live lemon and citron variety page either has a verified blurb or a logged
reason it does not. Shipped as a PR; deploy happens at the batch close-out.
