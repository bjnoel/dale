# 2026-06-12 — Variety descriptions: Annona group + mangosteen

Part of the treestock /variety "what's unique" blurb rollout. This window covered the
custard-apple (cherimoya/atemoya), sugar-apple, soursop, and mangosteen catalogues.

**Added 10 verified blurbs:**
- Custard apple: Fino de Jete, Bays, White (cherimoya cultivars).
- Mangosteen: Purple (the true Garcinia mangostana), Lemon Drop (G. intermedia),
  Yellow (umbrella name for G. dulcis / G. xanthochymus). Blurbs clarify that only
  the purple one is the true mangosteen.
- Soursop: Cuban Fiberless, Whitman Fibreless (the two documented fibreless lines).
- Sugar apple: Kampong Mauve, Purple (the purple-skinned Annona squamosa forms).
