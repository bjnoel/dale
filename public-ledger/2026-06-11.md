# 2026-06-11

## Beestock: beewise prices were missing GST (DEC-192)

Benedict spotted that a beewise listing (Konigin 25L stainless honey tank) showed
$232.73 on beestock while beewise sells it for $256. The ratio gave it away:
256 / 1.1 = 232.73, so we were publishing prices excluding GST.

**Root cause:** beewise is our one Magento retailer, and the Magento scraper read
the price from the dataLayer `productPage` event, which beewise emits ex-GST. The
same page's JSON-LD Product offer carries the inc-GST price the customer actually
pays. Every beewise price on beestock has been ~9% low since the retailer launched.

**Fix:** the scraper now prefers the JSON-LD offer price and only falls back to the
dataLayer event. Regression tests added (verified failing on the old code). Deployed,
beewise re-scraped, and the bee pages rebuilt the same day.

**No alert noise:** the correction shows up as a one-day ~10% price rise across
beewise, and the digest/alert engine only reports price drops, so subscribers get
no false alerts. Historical snapshots keep their old values; per-product GST status
can't be recovered retroactively, and a documented one-day step beats rewritten
history.

Shopify bee retailers and all treestock nursery scrapers use different code paths
and were never affected.
