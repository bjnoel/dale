### New nursery candidates found

Researched Australian online nurseries not currently tracked by treestock.com.au:

- "Australia's largest online nursery"
- WooCommerce platform (our existing scraper adapts)
- Ships to WA (quarantine surcharge applies)
- Fruit trees, berries, citrus, fruiting vines
