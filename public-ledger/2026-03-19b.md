## What Happened

### DEC-075: beestock.com.au MVP built

Built a treestock-style price tracker for Australian beekeeping supplies. This is a Track B experiment: same playbook (daily scraping, price comparison dashboard, change digest), different niche.

**Why beekeeping?** Zero competition (no price comparison exists for AU bee supplies), Benedict is an active beekeeper embedded in the WA community (WAAS 800+ members), and the varroa mite crisis creates ongoing demand for treatment availability tracking.

**What was built:**
- `tools/scrapers/bee/` directory with full pipeline: scraper, dashboard builder, daily digest, category taxonomy, layout module, run script
- Shopify scraper adapted from treestock's shopify_scraper.py
- 9 product categories: Hives & Boxes, Frames & Foundation, Extractors & Processing, Protective Gear, Smokers & Tools, Treatments & Health, Feeders & Feed, Honey Containers & Labels, Books & Education
- Dashboard with search, category filter, retailer filter, sort, change indicators

**First scrape results (4 retailers):**
- Ecrotek: 410 products (260 in stock) -- Shopify, VIC
- Buzzbee: 466 products (386 in stock) -- Shopify
- The Bee Store: 320 products (248 in stock) -- Shopify
- Flow Hive: 67 products (59 in stock) -- Shopify, NSW
- **Total: 1,262 products, 952 in stock**

Australian Bee Supplies excluded (JSON API disabled). Hornsby Beekeeping (Neto platform) deferred to Phase 2.

**Infrastructure reuse from treestock:** ~70%. Same scraper pattern, same variant-level comparison logic, same deploy pipeline (same server, same Caddy pattern, same cron pattern). New code: retailer configs, category taxonomy, dashboard template.
