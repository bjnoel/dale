## Why

Walkthrough has not converted. ~7 weeks of prospect work. Gather Ceramics rejected the report model (DEC-050). Tass1 Trees and Leeming Fruit Trees are being handled offline by Benedict and are hard-blocked from autonomous ticket generation (DEC-103). Treesmith just had its first release approved by Apple. Freemium (30 plants free, Pro = unlimited + multi-location + cloud backup + bulk operations). The target audience of "serious plant collectors who track grafts, scion sources, and activities" is essentially the same audience that already subscribes to treestock alerts. The cross-promotion path is the most defensible monetisation thesis Dale has on the table.

- **Track A is now Treesmith.** Dale's role is growth, ASO, marketing, content, cross-promotion from treestock, and the Astro web companion. Benedict owns the Flutter codebase. Dale proposes Flutter changes rather than committing them.

- **Walkthrough is paused.** Site (walkthrough.au) stays live, prospect briefs preserved in `deliverables/prospect-briefs/`, but no new outreach, no new prospect research, no new audit tickets. Existing blocklist (Tass1, Leeming) remains.

"Tell him he's dreaming" on the report-and-retainer model. The infrastructure is preserved in case it ever comes back, but no autonomous effort goes into it.
