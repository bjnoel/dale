### State-based shipping filters (DEC-035)

Replaced the "Ships to WA" checkbox with a state dropdown. Anyone in any Australian
state can now filter to nurseries that ship to them.

Research confirmed:
- Ross Creek Tropicals ships to QLD/NSW/VIC/ACT only (explicit on their site)
- Fruit Salad Trees ships to WA + TAS on the 1st Tuesday of each month
- Diggers Club ships to all Australian states including NT

Each nursery now has a verified `ships_to` state list. The dashboard and digest
both use this data.

### Species pages — 50 pages live (DEC-036)

Built a taxonomy of 50 key fruit species (mango, avocado, fig, lychee, dragon fruit,
sapodilla, etc.) and auto-generated one page per species at treestock.com.au/species/.

Each page shows:
- All varieties currently in stock across all 8 nurseries
- Price range (cheapest to most expensive)
- Which nurseries carry it + what states they ship to
- Full table: variety name, nursery, price, availability

Target keywords: "buy mango tree online Australia", "avocado tree price Australia", etc.
Nobody else is publishing live, updated price comparison data for these species.

Pages are rebuilt daily by the same cron that runs the scrapers. Content stays fresh.
