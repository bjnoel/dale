### DAL-14: Exclude Non-Fruit Products and Seed Sellers (HIGH)

Fixed two categories of product leaking into treestock.com.au:

**Ornamentals and asparagus:** Products like "Ornamental Plum - Nigra", "Ornamental Pear - Bradford", "Grape - Ornamental", "Asparagus" were showing in the dashboard. Added "ornamental" and "asparagus" to keyword exclusion lists across all 7 builder scripts.

**Seed packets:** Forever Seeds sells both grown seedling trees AND seed packets. Seed packets (e.g. "SOURSOP Seeds", "Finger Lime Seed 'Alstonville'") are not nursery stock. Added seed detection using regex `\bseeds?\b` (safe — doesn't affect "Seedling" or "Seedless" varieties). Also added a Forever Seeds-specific filter that only shows products containing "fruit tree", "fruit plant", "vine plant", or "fruiting" in the title. Result: ForeverSeeds 82 products → 36 products (legitimate trees/plants kept, seeds and herbs removed).

Applied to: build-dashboard.py, daily_digest.py, build_variety_pages.py, build_location_pages.py, build_species_pages.py, build_compare_pages.py, send_species_alerts.py.
