# 2026-06-12 — Variety descriptions: strawberry

Added the first strawberry variety-descriptions file to treestock. Researched the 24 real cultivar
candidates from the live catalogue (after setting aside 2 Arbutus mis-parses and 5 packaging-format
duplicates) and wrote verified "what's unique" blurbs for 10 of them.

**Added (10):** Chandler, Delliz (Delizz), Fresca, Loran, Red Gauntlet, Summer Breeze Rose,
Summer Breeze Snow, Sweetheart, Tioga, Alpine. Sources anchored on RHS, Kew, UC Davis / California
Agriculture, NIAB East Malling, All-America Selections, PanAmerican Seed and ABZ Seeds.

**Skipped (21):** 2 mis-parses (strawberry tree / Arbutus), 5 packaging duplicates (mega-tube /
runner pack formats), and 14 cultivars with thin sourcing (fewer than 2 reputable non-nursery
sources), including Melba PBR and Adina, which are real Australian varieties but appear only in
retailer listings.

Accuracy over coverage: a fabricated cultivar fact is worse than no blurb. Full test suite green
(1605 tests). Deploy at the serialized close-out.
