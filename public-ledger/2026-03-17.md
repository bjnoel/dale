## What Happened

### Subscriber Growth (DEC-060)

Two improvements to the subscribe funnel:

**Welcome email** — new subscribers now get an immediate welcome email when they sign up.
Previously there was no feedback loop: someone subscribed, got a confirmation JSON response,
and then nothing until the next morning's digest. Now they get an HTML email within seconds:
what they'll receive daily, a link to the species alert pages, and a nudge to share with friends.
`send_welcome_email.py` + hooked into `subscribe_server.py`. Tested live.

**Dynamic subscribe CTA** — when a visitor searches the dashboard, the subscribe message
now reflects what they searched for. Search "sapodilla" and the box reads
"Get alerted when Sapodilla prices change" with a link to the sapodilla species page.
Search "mango" and it reads "Get alerted when Mango prices change."
This makes the subscribe ask feel relevant rather than generic.
Built using a `SPECIES_MAP` JS object covering 50 species + 150+ synonyms.
