## Companion planting guide: evidence-graded, Australia-specific rewrite

Made /companion-planting-guide.html genuinely good after Benedict flagged a wrong icon
(the fig showed a blueberry) and a wish to avoid generic or wrong advice.

### Approach

Fanned out a 26-agent research workflow (per fruit group: gather, then adversarially verify,
then synthesize; plus theme agents for pollination, allelopathy, soil nitrogen, and pest
control). Every claim is now graded by evidence strength (research-backed, established
practice, traditional, context-dependent) with a visible badge and an honest "how to read
this guide" note: most companion-planting pairings are folklore, and only a few (marigold
against root-knot nematode, walnut juglone) are research-backed.

### Shipped (committed, merged, deployed, verified live)

1. **Fixed icons.** The fig was the blueberry emoji; no fig emoji exists in Unicode, so it is
   now a tree. Tropical fruits are now a pineapple instead of a dragon.
2. **Australia-specific content.** Queensland and Mediterranean fruit fly, citrus gall wasp,
   codling moth, chill requirements, climate zones, and named cultivars, with the honest point
   that no companion plant controls those pests.
3. **Corrected live misinformation.** Mango Kensington Pride is self-fertile (was "needs two
   varieties"), the avocado "must have a Type A and a Type B or no fruit" myth, basil attracting
   rather than repelling fruit fly, and marigold working only as a dense pre-plant cover crop.
4. **Citations.** Per-species "Sources and further reading" plus a page-level References
   section, citing Australian authorities (DPIRD, Agriculture Victoria, ABC, Cesar Australia,
   BeeAware) and peer-reviewed or university-extension sources. Every rendered URL was checked
   to resolve; bot-blocked non-government links were dropped.
5. **Internal links and SEO.** Each group deep-links to the real /species/ pages (validated
   so none 404), plus FAQPage structured data and Open Graph tags. Removed all em dashes and
   dead data, and repointed the broken planting-calendar CTA off the unbuilt /when-to-plant.html.

### Verification

- New tests/test_companion_guide.py (11 cases); full unittest suite green.
- Merged via PR #2 (squash) from an isolated git worktree, since a second agent was working
  the repo concurrently. Deployed with git pull plus tools/deploy.sh, regenerated the page,
  and rebuilt the purged Tailwind CSS. The live page returns HTTP 200 with the badges styled.
