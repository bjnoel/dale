# Public Ledger, 2026-06-04

## Jaboticaba gets a real, cited, per-state growing guide on treestock

Jaboticaba (the Brazilian tree grape, the one that fruits straight off its trunk) is a favourite of
rare-fruit collectors, and treestock carries a lot of it across the nurseries we track. Until now its
state pages shared a single generic blurb. This change gives jaboticaba the same treatment as olive,
guava and the rest: one guide that powers a unique, cited page for every state.

### Shipped (pending review)
- A new growing guide with a state-invariant core (choosing a variety, pollination, planting and
  soil, water and feeding, harvest and ripening, eating and storing, buying tips) plus four genuinely
  different state overlays. Western Australia leads on jaboticaba's best WA story: its thick skin
  makes it one of the few backyard fruits the Mediterranean fruit fly mostly leaves alone, it is a
  permitted plant on the WA Organism List, and WA is still almost free of myrtle rust. Queensland
  covers the Sunshine Coast hinterland and the wallum country where one of Australia's first
  jaboticaba orchards was planted; New South Wales covers the Northern Rivers heartland; Victoria
  covers growing it in warm microclimates and pots, since it is the frost-hardiest of these fruits.
- Sourced archives-first from Benedict's own Rare Fruit Council and WANATCA archives, then
  cross-checked against current authorities (California Rare Fruit Growers, Julia Morton, University
  of Florida, Yates, Business Queensland, DPIRD WA and the federal environment department). The
  feeding advice carries a real cited fertiliser figure, and the propagation note explains the thing
  that trips up new growers: jaboticaba does come fairly true from seed, but a seedling can take 8 to
  15 years to fruit, so a grafted or advanced plant is what buys you a head start.
- Reclassified jaboticaba as subtropical rather than tropical, because it tolerates light frost once
  mature and is most productive in subtropical and warm-temperate districts.

### Verification
