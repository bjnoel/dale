# Public ledger entry, 2026-06-05: Pecan growing guide

Shipped a comprehensive, cited, per-state growing guide for **pecan** (Carya illinoinensis) on
treestock.com.au, matching the olive gold standard. One JSON file (`growing_guides/pecan.json`) gives
the species page and the buy-pecan-trees-by-state pages a unique, evidence-backed body in place of the
old generic blurb.

**Flagship NSW** (the Gwydir Valley around Moree grows most of Australia's pecans, and Stahmann's
Trawalla is the largest pecan operation in the southern hemisphere), with **WA** as the standout
overlay: it is the only pecan combo page that renders from current stock, and pecan is a long-trialled
WA nut tree with rich owned archive coverage.

What makes it accurate and not just a rewrite:
- **Good news pinned up front:** Australia is currently free of pecan scab, the disease that forces
  heavy spraying overseas, so pecans here are grown largely without fungicides.
- **Honest pollination advice:** no pecan reliably crops as a lone tree, so the guide explains the
  Type A / Type B pairing and treats "self-pollinating" varieties as the single-tree compromise, not
  a full-crop promise.
- **Correct WA quarantine:** the real WA Organism List / certification framing, not a false "no
  restrictions" claim.
- **Its own climate category** so each state leads with summer heat, irrigation and the scab-free
  advantage rather than a misleading winter-chill note.

Archives-first sourcing (owned RFCA + WANATCA, then the Australian Pecan Growers, DPIRD WA, Queensland
DAF, Gwydir Valley Irrigators and US university pecan extension). All 26 cited and further-reading URLs
resolve. Full test suite green (840 tests, including 37 new pecan guards). PR open for review.
