# 2026-06-08 — Variety descriptions: apple + pomelo

Added verified "what's unique about this variety" blurbs to the treestock `/variety` pages for
apple and pomelo, part of the DEC-178 rollout (branch `dale/varieties-apple-pomelo`).

**Apple (now 40 described, 9 skipped, ~101 remaining of 150 live):** added 34 cultivars across
commercial (Golden Delicious, Fuji, Jonathan, Sundowner, Jonagold, Mutsu, McIntosh), low-chill and
Australian/NZ (Tropic Sweet, Tropical Beauty, Lady Williams, Bonza, Democrat, Monty's Surprise),
heritage dessert (Gravenstein, Cox's Orange Pippin, Rome Beauty, Peasgood's Nonsuch, Cornish
Aromatic, Geeveston Fanny, Orleans Reinette), modern disease-resistant (Crimson Crisp, Pixie
Crunch, Spartan, Empire, Akane, King David, Twenty Ounce) and traditional cider (Dabinett, Michelin,
Somerset Redstreak, Brown Snout, Tremlett's Bitter, Stoke Red, Bulmer's Norman) varieties.

**Pomelo (1 described, 8 skipped, 3 remaining of 12 live):** only Nam Roi (a documented Vietnamese
cultivar) cleared the two-source bar; the rest are nursery trade names with no independent source.

Every blurb is verified against at least two reputable sources (at least one non-nursery), with a
stored claims-to-sources ledger and no fabricated figures. Skips were recorded for clear mis-parses
(Malay apple, Kei apple, Wood apple, sugar apples and wax apples grouped under "Apple", Fraser Island
which is the native Acronychia, and the pomelo "tangelo" which is a different hybrid). Tests green;
shipped via PR, not yet deployed.
