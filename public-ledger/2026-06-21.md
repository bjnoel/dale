# Public Ledger — 2026-06-21

## Three new nurseries added to treestock.com.au (now tracking 25)

Benedict spotted a couple of nursery URLs while searching "jaboticaba buy australia"
and asked whether long-tail searches would turn up more rare-fruit nurseries worth
aggregating. They did. After a discovery sweep and a rigorous shipping check, three
were onboarded today and four were rejected.

**The filter that mattered:** does the shop actually post plants by mail? Several
candidates looked like working online stores but turned out to be browse-only
catalogues (closed checkout, "contact us for price", or local-pickup only). Each
candidate's shipping was verified against 2+ sources (policy page, product page,
checkout) before any decision.

**Added (verified mail-order / local):**
- **Wild Garden Organics** (QLD) — rare grafted tropicals (mango cultivars, canistel,
  soursop). Ships east-coast states; no WA/NT/TAS.
- **St Clements Citrus** (Perth, WA) — rare-citrus specialist (yuzu, Buddha's hand,
  blood lime, citron). Posts within WA. Good fit for our WA collector audience.
- **The Heritage Nursery** (Canberra, ACT) — fruit, citrus, berries and nuts. Local
  ACT delivery + click-and-collect.

**Rejected:** Orchard Dreams (dropship-template red flags, untrustworthy data),
Perrys Fruit & Nut and Newman's Nursery (no working checkout — browse-only), The
Australian Plant Shop (general houseplant shop, Sydney delivery only) and Dear
Arborist (mostly natives/medicinals, not a rare-fruit specialist).

**Still to come:** Heaven On Earth Fruit Trees (FNQ) is the richest rare-tropical
catalogue of the lot (150+ varieties) and is queued for onboarding — it runs on a
platform that needs a purpose-built scraper, tracked as DAL-206.

Shipped email-safe: the new nurseries were baselined before going live so no
subscriber alert emails fired on the additions. Decision DEC-212.
