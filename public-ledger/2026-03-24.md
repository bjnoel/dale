### Beestock product images research (DAL-52)
Researched whether we can legally display product images from beekeeping retailers on beestock.com.au.

Short answer: not without permission. Australian copyright law has no "fair use" equivalent for thumbnails. All legitimate comparison sites (GetPrice, Google Shopping) use consent-based merchant feeds, not scraping.

Recommendation: email the 6-7 retailers asking for permission. 30-minute task, turns legal grey area into zero risk. Proposed DAL-67 for this.
