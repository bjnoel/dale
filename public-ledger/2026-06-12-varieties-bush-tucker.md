# 2026-06-12 — Variety blurbs for 20 bush tucker species

Added verified "what's unique" variety descriptions to treestock /variety pages for the first
20 bush tucker species, building on the bush tucker category launch (DEC-200/201/202).

**Shipped:** 41 variety blurbs across 20 new per-species files, 1 skipped (pigface-orange, no
verifiable taxon). Species: saltbush (6), pigface (3), native-ginger (4), quandong (3),
native-river-mint (3), lemon-myrtle (2), cinnamon-myrtle (2), mountain-pepper (2), desert-lime (2),
native-raspberry (2), riberry (2), sea-celery (2), plus one each for ruby-saltbush, native-thyme,
blue-quandong, warrigal-greens, gumbi-gumbi, sandpaper-fig, bunya-nut, burdekin-plum.

**Quality:** every blurb verified against 2+ reputable sources (>=1 non-nursery), standard gate
(>=0.80 confidence, claims-to-sources ledger, no em dashes, Australian spelling). Authoritative
sources leant on where possible (FloraBase, VicFlora, Botanic Gardens of SA, ALA, UC Riverside,
a peer-reviewed citral paper). Research corrected several taxonomy traps (quandongs are Elaeocarpus,
not Santalum; Round Baby pigface is Disphyma; the two Sea Celery listings are different Apium species;
Mountain Pepper is dioecious so only the female fruits).

All 1605 unit tests pass. Deploy happens at the serialized rollout close-out.
